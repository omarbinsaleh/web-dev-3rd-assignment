Why It Is Important To Combine Exterior And Interior Design

One of the main reasons why it is important to combine exterior and interior design is because it creates a sense of flow. When you enter a home, you should feel like you are entering into one cohesive space. If the exterior and interior design of your home is not harmonious, it can feel like you are walking into two different homes. The best way to create a sense of flow is to choose a theme or style for your home and carry it throughout both the interior and exterior.
